## PM Takeaways
- Early cohorts show steady month-one retention, with some improvement in later periods.
- The most recent cohorts have smaller sample sizes, so the percentages should be interpreted cautiously.
- The data suggests repeat engagement is present, but deeper month-over-month retention is still uneven.
